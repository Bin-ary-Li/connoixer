About ConnoiXer

ConnoiXer is an interdisciplinary project that utilizes state-of-the-art machine learning technology to examine arts, beauty, and aesthetics. It employs numerous neural network models that are trained on open-source datasets. The project is still on-going and improving. At its fruition, ConnoiXer will be able to fulfill tasks such as aesthetic value evaluation, painting style/genre classification, and art auctioned price assessment.

How to use

In current version, user will be able to submit his/her image at the main page of this website. The image will be sent to a trained convolutional neural network (CNN) model to judge its aesthetic value (on a scale from 0 to 100, with 100 being the best). Your uploaded images will be deleted after model finishing processing. Please give it try and experience it yourself.

More features are coming in the future.

About Dataset

This version of ConnoiXer takes advantage of a research-oriented database called Jenaesthetic that created by the researchers at the University of Jena.

Quoted from their website:

"The dataset consists of 1628 highly aesthetic painting from 11 different western art periods. 16 different keywords have been selected to cover most of the common subject matters in a painting. For each painting, 1-3 subject matter keywords have been assigned in the order of their impact on the visual appearance of the painting."
